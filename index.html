Our Team

Umar

Umar has over 15 years of experience in the barbering industry. He specializes in classic and modern haircuts, beard grooming, and straight razor shaves. His precision and attention to detail have earned him a loyal clientele.

Jamal

Jamal has been a barber for 10 years and is known for his creative and edgy styles. He excels in fades, undercuts, and intricate designs. Jamal’s passion for her craft shines through in every cut.

Michael

Michael brings 12 years of barbering experience to Ice Cuts. He’s known for his professionalism and versatility, handling everything from traditional cuts to trendy styles with ease. Michael is also great with kids, making him a favorite among families.

James

With 20 years of experience, James is a seasoned barber who excels in both classic and contemporary styles. His extensive knowledge of barbering techniques and his ability to tailor cuts to each client’s needs make him a cornerstone of Ice Cuts.

Charles

Charles has been a barber for 8 years and is renowned for his flawless fades and sharp line-ups. His expertise in various hair textures and styles makes him a versatile and highly sought-after barber.
